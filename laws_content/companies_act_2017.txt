COMPANIES ACT, 2017
(ACT NO. XIX OF 2017)

[30th May, 2017]

An Act to consolidate and amend the law relating to companies and for matters connected therewith and incidental thereto...

PART I - PRELIMINARY

1. Short title, extent and commencement.—
(1) This Act may be called the Companies Act, 2017.
(2) It extends to the whole of Pakistan.
(3) This section and section 508 shall come into force at once and the remaining provisions shall come into force on such date as the Federal Government may, by notification in the official Gazette, appoint.

PART II - CONSTITUTION AND INCORPORATION OF COMPANIES

11. Mode of forming a company.—
(1) Any three or more persons associated for any lawful purpose may, by subscribing their names to a memorandum of association and complying with the requirements of this Act in respect of registration, form a public company...
