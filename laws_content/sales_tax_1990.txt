SALES TAX ACT, 1990
(ACT NO. VII OF 1990)
AS AMENDED BY FINANCE ACT, 2024

[1st July, 1990]

An Act to consolidate and amend the law relating to the levy and collection of sales tax...

CHAPTER I - PRELIMINARY

1. Short title, extent and commencement.—
(1) This Act may be called the Sales Tax Act, 1990.
(2) It extends to the whole of Pakistan.
(3) It shall come into force on such date as the Federal Government may, by notification in the official Gazette, appoint...

CHAPTER II - SCOPE AND PAYMENT OF TAX

3. Scope of tax.—
(1) Subject to the provisions of this Act, there shall be charged, levied and paid a tax known as sales tax at the rate of eighteen per cent of the value of taxable supplies made by a registered person in the course or furtherance of any taxable activity carried on by him; and goods imported into Pakistan.
